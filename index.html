Information theory

LDPC Codes - Creation of a python package "pyLDPC" to simulate coding and decoding of text, sound and image files when transmitted through a GWN (gaussian white noise) channel. A beautiful mixture of linear algebra and probability theory. The package and several tutorials are available at PyPI .
